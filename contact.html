❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add movies to my watchlist?

First login to your account. Then on any movie or TV show card you will see a + button in the top right corner, click it to save. You can also click a card to open full details and use the "Add to Watchlist" button there. You must be logged in to use the watchlist.

Is my watchlist saved?

Yes! Your watchlist is saved privately to your account in your browser. Each user has their own separate watchlist. Note that data is stored locally so clearing your browser data will remove it.

Where does the movie data come from?

All movie and TV show information is provided by The Movie Database (TMDB) API, ensuring you get accurate and up-to-date information.

Do I need an account to use CineTrack?

You can browse and search movies without an account. However, to save items to your watchlist you need to register and login. Registration is completely free and only takes a few seconds.

Is CineTrack free to use?

Absolutely! CineTrack is a free university project created for educational purposes. All features are available at no cost.
