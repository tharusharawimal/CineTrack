ℹ️ About CineTrack

Welcome to CineTrack! Your ultimate destination for discovering trending movies and TV series. We provide comprehensive information, ratings, and a personal watchlist to help you keep track of what you want to watch.

Our Mission

CineTrack was created to solve the common problem of content overload. With countless streaming platforms and new releases every week, it's easy to lose track of what you want to watch. Our platform brings everything together in one place, making it simple to discover, track, and organize your entertainment.

Simplify Your Entertainment

No more forgetting about that movie your friend recommended or losing track of new seasons of your favorite shows.

✨ Key Features

Trending Content

Stay up-to-date with what's trending right now in movies and TV shows.

Advanced Search

Find exactly what you're looking for with our powerful search functionality.

Personal Watchlist

Save movies and shows to your own watchlist. Each user has a separate watchlist tied to their account.

User Accounts

Register and login to get your own personal account. Your watchlist is saved privately under your account.

Ratings & Reviews

View TMDB ratings and comprehensive information before you watch.

Detailed Information

Get complete details including cast, genres, and plot summaries.

Responsive Design

Access CineTrack seamlessly on any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile.

❓ How to Use CineTrack

1
Register

Create a free account using your username and email

2
Login

Sign in to access your personal watchlist and features

3
Browse & Search

Explore trending, top-rated content or search for anything specific

4

5
Manage

🛠️ Built With

Frontend Technologies
  • HTML5 for semantic structure
  • CSS3 for custom styling and animations
  • Bootstrap 5 for responsive design
  • JavaScript ES6+ for interactivity
Data & Storage
  • TMDB API for movie/TV show data
  • Local Storage for user accounts & watchlists
  • Per-user watchlist storage system
  • RESTful API integration
  • Asynchronous data fetching

University Project

This project was created as part of the ICT 2204 - Web Design and Technologies course at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, Department of Computing. It demonstrates proficiency in modern web development technologies including HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript, TMDB API integration, user authentication with session management, and per-user data storage.
